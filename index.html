Statistics

54% of Middle and High School students in NCSD are invlolved in some athletic program, including soccer, basketball, volleyball, and football teams.

29% of students are involved in musical arts like orchestra, band, choir and theatrical arts like drama and dance.

22 AP (Advanced Placement) courses and 15 IB (International Baccalaureate) are offered at NCSD, including history, math, computer science, etc.

NCSD is a 1 for 1 learning environment. Students in Kindergarten and 1st Grade are offered an ipad to access certain learning tools, research and read e-books on, and take standardized tests. kids in grades 2-12 are offered a chromebook to learn with.

75 different languages and dialects are spoken in NCSD, like Hindi, Japanese, and Spanish, making us a diverse community.

The graduation rate of high school students in the 2018-19 school year was 96%, far above the state median of 82%.

185 clubs, STEM programs, and sports teams are available to join, some of which are the brainchild of enthusiastic students and volunteers.
